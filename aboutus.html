About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Resues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitation Association. "National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.">

Watch the video below to meet our new, rescued friends.

We help many animals, such as:
  • Racoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Did you find an injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
